About us

Turquoise Waterfront Hotel is a modern and elegant 5 star hotel that has a total of 80 rooms, two restaurants and three pools. Each room is beautifully decorated, has an inviting feeling, a gorgeous view and a private bathroom that is fully equipped to fulfill all of your needs. All of the areas of our hotel has fast Wi-fi.
When you book your stay you can opt for the all-inclusive package which provides you with three meals/day in both of our restaurants at any hour. In your free time you can opt to walk to the beach or enjoy our pools: the rooftop pool, the courtyard pool or the interior pool. 

types of rooms

The Double King Rooms

The rooms have a spacious king sized bed and have a cozy,  comfortable and relaxed atmosphere.

The Twin Turquoise Rooms

The rooms have two twin beds. You can opt for a room that has two single sized or two queen sized beds.

The Single Bed Rooms

The rooms offer a single bed and are perfect for people that want to have their own spacious room.

The Four People  Rooms

The rooms have a king sized bed and an expandable couch that can sleep another two people.
